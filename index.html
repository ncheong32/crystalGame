CrystalsCollector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game in the green box below.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific number of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number generated below, and you lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each crystal is not known until you click on each one of them.

Every time you restart the game, the number of points assigned to each crystal changes.
